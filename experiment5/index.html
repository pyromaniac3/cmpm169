I figured this has a solid base that I can then comb through and make something more engaging!

Integrate

With those as my two inspirations, I am setting off to see how I can combine it. I am planning on having the cube be my base.
My initial goals are to be able to : [] Spin the Box [] Click to Shake the Box [] Change its shape [stretch]

To sum it up, I was able to spin the box, but shaking was out of my leauge. I tried to do some weird translation thing, but it just was not working out for me

Then when I tried to make the balls have some physcis to them, it was even harder. I struggled to make it happen, and was getting too frusterated. After an hour or two of attempts, I decided to scrap the whole idea and go in a completely different more interesting direction.

Innovate

Using what I learned from my other sections, I decided to find a 3D model of shrek onlin, because it was the first character that came to my mind. Searching on sketchfab I found a good model from HarrisonHag1 and I decided to roll with it.

I put him in the cube, gave him the normal material and kept the ADD blend mode and thought about what I could do.

The first thing to come to my mind was to put him in his swamp. I figured, he'd be spinning around because he was getting really mad at you. Why does shrek get mad? Cause you are in his swamp. With a bit of tinting, and a couple of extra shreks thrown in the mix. I had a piece of art.
